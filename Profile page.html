About Me

Hi I am Rahul a Toronto based UX/UI designer who is also a sucker for stories. I like bridging gap’s between problems and solutions.I enjoy learning about human psychology which helps me focusing on creating user-centric designs while keeping empathy. Believing in no frustrations designs, where others may use meditation as a source of keeping themselves calm I use sketching and photography as my source.

My Skills

User Research and Empathy

There’s no style over substance. Laying user research with past, present and future references.

Pencil and Digital wireframing

Not reinventing the wheel here but using inspirations from previous work, nature and daily routine. Putting down ideas on paper brings them a certainty of motion.

Micro Interaction Design

Running electricity to my Frankenstein. Colours, Typography and micro-interactions with great quality photographs help me build the style guide, which results in building my designs digitally.
